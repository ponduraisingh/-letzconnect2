LetzConnect launches YES Program

Jun 24 2022

What if the youth of India could be empowered to validate good ideas for launching entrepreneurial ventures ?

What if that empowerment began within higher education institutions ?

And what if the tools and techniques for such empowerment was part of a structured online curriculum ?

Empowered Startups is based in Vancouver, Canada. They mentor, accelerate and incubate innovative domestic and global business ideas. Empowered Startups, work with both aspiring and driven entrepreneurial leaders capable of building outstanding startups that enhance the prosperity of their chosen ecosystem.

Yes Program has been developed and designed by Empowered Startups and now brings to the youth of India, the first of its kind program to inspire and educate youth to be entrepreneurial leaders.

The structured program teaches students the business acumen, critical thinking and essential knowledge required to turn their ideas into viable business ideas.

LETZCONNECT® launches YES Progam, a 100% online Entrepreneurial Learning program for college and university students in India !

  • Designed and developed by EMPOWERED STARTUPS a Global Business Incubator
  • Turn-key, digitally delivered state-of-the-art Curriculum and Learning Activities
  • Built-in teacher insights, quizzes, and assessments for ongoing student evaluation
  • Structured into 8 modules, with a 70% minimum pass-score required in each module

The YES Program is a unique opportunity for the Youth of India where they can chart a full entrepreneurial journey with cutting-edge structured online content. Students can produce a viable Venture Plan that is ready to pitch for Startup funding. A Global community of Mentors is available to validate business ideas and students can build a Personal Profile for Higher Studies or Employment. Students will receive a Certificate from a Global Business Incubator.

Also, students receive immediate recognition on LinkedIn with an authenticated Certificate posted on their profile.
