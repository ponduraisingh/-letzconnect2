Education Digitized

Sep 24 2022

Like every other college student, I strive to juggle both co-curricular and academic activities like a pro through every semester. But it ain’t that easy, is it? Assignments, seminars, and presentations pile up one after the other with deadlines cramming into one single day. Oh! And not to forget those missed out lecture notes.

How to catch up with the steady flow of syllabus despite your absence is something that every student struggles with.

Here is where technology steps in and helps process tonnes of information generated in the education fraternity, making a student’s life much easier.

This is where LetzConnect plays a major role, being a new age digital platform that combines all my learning needs- from the personalised digital bookshelf to intuitive system that makes specific suggestions based on my interests. LetzConnect is indeed a homogenous platform that has helped me immensely at college.
