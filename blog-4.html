MHRD announces NEAT scheme for better learning outcomes in higher education

Aug 8 2022

LetzConnect has been selected along with a few other companies to be a part of the NEAT program of MHRD. The program recognises that AI can be used to make learning more personalised and customised as per the requirements of the learner.

MHRD has partnered with Ed-Tech companies that offer certifications in highly marketable skills. MHRD is partnering through the selected Ed Tech companies and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) will act as the implementation agency.

LetzConnect is proud to be a part of this endeavour and all of LetzConnect offerings will be available on the NEAT portal.

The NEAT portal is to be launched by the Hon. Minister in Jan 2020.
