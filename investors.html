Investors

LetzConnect commenced its operations in 2016 and it is currently India's fastest growing student ecosystem. Currently having completed Pre Series A, LetzConnect is now Government accredited, by both AICTE and MHRD. We believe that with like-minded visionaries, LetzConnect is poised to grow exponentially.


  • Conceptualisation of LetzConnect as an idea to connect students on a single platform, bringing together the various functions onto a single platform for educational and holistic development.

  • April of 2016 - Official incorporation of LetzConnect Technologies Pvt Ltd.

  • Development of the IOS, Android and Web platforms.

  • Launched across 150 colleges, accessed by 1,80,000 users with an active DAU of 87%.

    • AICTE launches LetzConnect for Campus Digitization across their 10,400 colleges; 3000 colleges registered.
    • Pre Series A funding secured.
