Artificial Intelligence within LetzConnect

Jul 18 2022

Artificial Intelligence has enhanced the functionality of applications across several domains. AI Labs, an initiative of LetzConnect Technologies Pvt Ltd., has been launched with the objective of solving the problem of employability and learnability using the tools that AI has to offer. We, at AI Labs, are determined to uplift the student community by providing avenues for skill, knowledge, and personality development. The AI-powered LetzConnect application helps students from the inception of their college life until they have secured jobs in their field of interest. LetzConnect provides solutions to a wide range of problems with the help of its highly efficient AI.

In order for students to perform well in their area of study, there has to be access to quality content which, not only gives them the required knowledge but also inspires them to pursue the said field. LetzConnect is dedicated to providing the right content to the right user with the help of the in-build recommendation engine powered by the AI. Students will be encouraged by the LetzConnect chatbot PAL, to pursue courses or internships in their line of study by analyzing their interests. The Individual Aspiration Mapping (IAM), an index for analytics introduced by LetzConnect will continuously help students in analyzing their capability and skills, motivating them to improve their profile. These statistics will, in turn, help companies to determine talented individuals for recruitment.

The AI of LetzConnect offers several solutions in the form of personalized recommendations and suggestions by the chatbot - PAL, a scorecard of all the skills and capabilities of students displayed in the User Analytics Dashboard, auto sign-in and attendance maintenance with the help of the facial recognition feature, content searching and sharing with the help of speech recognition, among many others. LetzConnect offers employers critical hiring data to detect top talent and provides personalized job roles, salary information, benefits and more, to help students in their academic and professional paths.

LetzConnect will expose students to a wide variety of relevant events, internships, and content that will improve their knowledge. Every student will get a personalized flexible path of study after being analyzed by the AI. LetzConnect strives for continuous skill development in order to attract employers and to accelerate each student’s career. Several policies and Programmes pertaining to each student will be recommended to avail full benefits of government and private schemes. The problem of employment, that has been persisting for way too long all across the world, now, has a solution in the form of LetzConnect.
